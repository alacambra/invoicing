About me

My name is Albert Lacambra Basil. I have over 10 years of experience working as a lead developer & team lead delivering complex business solutions to a variety of prominent German corporations in the financial & logistics sector and in startup environment with Scrum, Kanban & Rapid Prototyping.

My core expertise is with the Java EE stack, CI/CD, AWS, Docker, Unix Networking, graph theory, graph databases and Hyperledger Fabric.

The last two years I have begun to work as a consultant helping companies to solve their specific challenges.

I am also an active contributor on the Hyperledger Fabric Java Chaincode project. This project allows the development of chaincode for the Fabric platform using the Java language.
